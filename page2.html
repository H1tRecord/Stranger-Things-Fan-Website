Season 1

Set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, Stranger Things Season 1 follows the mysterious disappearance of a young boy named Will Byers. As his friends and family search for him, they uncover a series of supernatural mysteries, including a girl with psychokinetic abilities known as Eleven. The group encounters government experiments, parallel dimensions, and a monstrous creature from the Upside Down. Filled with suspense, friendship, and 80s nostalgia, Season 1 explores the thin line between reality and the unknown.

Main Cast:

  • Eleven: A young girl with telekinetic and psychokinetic abilities raised in Hawkins National Laboratory.
  • Mike Wheeler: One of Will's best friends and El's love interest.
  • Dustin Henderson: Another of Will's friends, known for his love of science and adventure.
  • Lucas Sinclair: Will's best friend and the voice of reason in the group.
  • Will Byers: The boy who gets trapped in the Upside Down.
  • Joyce Byers: Will's determined mother.
  • Jim Hopper:The gruff but caring chief of Hawkins police.

Supporting Cast:

  • Jonathan Byers: Will's older brother, protective and artistic.
  • Nancy Wheeler: Mike's older sister, drawn to Jonathan and investigating the Upside Down.
  • Dr. Martin Brenner: The director of Hawkins National Laboratory and Eleven's former caretaker.
  • Barb Holland: Nancy's best friend, who mysteriously disappears.
  • Eleven's Mother: A woman with telekinetic abilities who was experimented on at Hawkins Lab.

Season 2

A year after Will's return from the Upside Down, Hawkins faces new threats. A shadowy monster lurks, infecting townsfolk and spreading a web of darkness. Eleven, weakened after closing the gate, struggles to regain her powers. Meanwhile, new student Max Mayfield brings her own baggage, haunted by a mysterious past. As old friendships are tested and new alliances forged, the kids face familiar horrors and uncover shocking truths about Hawkins' hidden secrets.

Main Cast:

  • Same as Season 1, plus:
  • Max Mayfield: Lucas's new stepsister, tough and independent.
  • Will Byers: Now back from the Upside Down, but haunted by his experiences.

Supporting Cast:

  • Billy Hargrove: Max's abusive stepbrother, connected to the Upside Down entity.
  • Bob Newby: Joyce's supportive boyfriend, killed by the Demogorgon.
  • Dr. Sam Owens: The new director of Hawkins Lab, more humane than Brenner.

Season 3

It's the summer of 1985, and Hawkins is basking in the joys of a new mall, Starcourt. But beneath the neon lights and carefree fun, a sinister plot unfolds. The Russians have reopened the gate to the Upside Down, seeking to exploit its power for their own gain. Meanwhile, Eleven struggles to adapt to life without her powers, navigating teenage romance and growing pains. As the Mind Flayer returns, stronger and more dangerous than ever, the gang must band together once more to save Hawkins from total annihilation.

Main Cast:

  • Same as Season 2, plus:
  • Robin Buckley: Steve Harrington's co-worker and eventual love interest.
  • Erica Sinclair: Lucas's younger sister, sassy and resourceful.

Supporting Cast:

  • Billy Hargrove: Now possessed by the Mind Flayer.
  • Jim Hopper: Now the mayor of Hawkins, struggling with his new role.
  • Dr. Alexei: A Russian scientist who helps Hopper close the gate to the Upside Down.

Season 4

Six months have passed since the Battle of Starcourt, and Hawkins is reeling from the loss of Hopper and Billy. Will still suffers from the lingering effects of the Upside Down, haunted by visions of a terrifying new creature. Meanwhile, a series of brutal murders rocks the town, drawing the attention of a charismatic yet troubled metalhead named Eddie Munson. As the kids unravel the mystery, they discover a hidden dimension called the Creel House, gateway to a horrifying entity known as Vecna.

Main Cast:

  • Same as Season 3, plus:
  • Murray Bauman: A conspiracy theorist who helps Joyce and Hopper.
  • Eddie Munson: The leader of Hawkins High's Hellfire Club, embroiled in the Upside Down mystery.
  • Argyle: Jonathan's new friend in California, with a laid-back personality.

Supporting Cast:

  • Vecna: The true identity of the Upside Down entity, manipulating Billy and others.
  • Victor Creel: A man who encountered Vecna years ago and left clues to his return.
  • Robin Buckley: Now working at a video store and exploring her sexuality.
